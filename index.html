Business

Agriculture, production of poultry, manufacturing of animal feed and food processing.

Overview

Tanmiah Food Company was founded in Riyadh in 1991 with capital of SAR 3 million. The company operates in three core segments: poultry production sector, additional food processing sector, and feed and veterinary services Sector. The company produces, processes, and sells fresh poultry products and other meat products, in addition to producing and selling animals feed, broiler chicks, hatching eggs, and animal health products. The company also implements comprehensive poultry and house projects, in addition to sale of poultry and house equipment.
